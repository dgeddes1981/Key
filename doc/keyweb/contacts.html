contacts

We are always interested in your submissions towards Eclipse and welcome them: be they poems, graphics, tinyfugue scripts or even humorous quotes. We'll accept anything.

Naturally, we want to know what you would like to see in Eclipse so feel free to make suggestions regarding future direction of Eclipse, including commands, concepts, help files or other items of interest.

The appropriate email address is forest@realm.progsoc.uts.edu.au and that is to where all submissions should go.
